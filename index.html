About

Hello! My name is Kim Brandt and I'm an aspiring graphic designer and web developer residing in Seattle.

I created my first website at the age of 12, and have always been strongly passionate about digital arts, the web, and photography. Currently at age 26, I am a graduate of Western Washington University with a major in Communications and Internet Studies looking to expand my design skills and experience as a freelancer. I have worked with various local clients providing them with websites for small businesses and organizations, posters and flyers for events, and other web and print solutions.

Technical skills: (x)HTML, CSS, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign & Dreamweaver, some PHP, SQL & JavaScript
